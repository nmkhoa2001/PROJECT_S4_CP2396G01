Setting up the dining table at QAT . Vegan Restaurant

  • Kaya
  • 03 Comments

You are looking to set up tables and chairs for your restaurant so that it is simple, polite but still luxurious and cost-effective. Please refer to the image of the installation of the dining table at the QAT vegan restaurant by Kaya furniture for more ideas.

QAT vegan restaurant is one of the famous and familiar brands for vegetarians. The space is relatively spacious, decorated in a simple but polite style. Kaya captures the characteristic that when designing vegan restaurants, it is not necessary to be lavish and flashy, but towards peace and purity to exude the spirit and spirit of Buddhism.

Most of the vegetarian restaurants have a light, simple, rustic but sophisticated design to give customers a comfortable and relaxing experience. Moreover, with the desire of customers to bring a close and cozy space, Kaya advised customers to choose the appropriate table and chair models.

After discussing and coming to an agreement with the customer, Kaya Furniture continued to install 25 sets of tables and chairs including LUNAR chairs, HANA chairs; DOXA chair, rubber wood. Let's take a look at these tables and chairs in terms of design, color, and material.

True to the design criteria for a vegetarian restaurant, Kaya chooses simple, elegant but still polite table and chair models, and especially brings a comfortable and pleasant feeling for customers to sit and eat. Stylized square table, beautifully rounded chairs and in harmony with the space. The size of the tables and chairs is also moderate, spacious enough for guests to sit and use, and suitable for the space of the restaurant.
